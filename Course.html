Welcome to course section of Jain University

Jain University Bangalore, through various schools of study, several centres, and 25 departments, offers UG, PG, Vocational, PhD, and other courses to students. These courses are provided across the Design, Engineering, Management, and other streams. Jain Deemed to be University, established in 1990, is accredited by NBA and A++ Grade by NAAC. The university also offers distance education through the Center for Distance & Online Education. Jain University Bangalore is a member of AIU, AUAP, NIBES, and the Association of Commonwealth Universities, and is approved by UGC. The Jain Deemed to be University is ranked 85 by NIRF 2023 under the Management category.
